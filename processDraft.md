## Purpose

It can be challenging to get into the gaming industry, and sometimes, having an impressive portfolio can be much better than only having a resume. Recruiters prefer candidates who show their expertise through action rather than words. The portfolio showcases a person’s technical proficiency, design skills, problem-solving process, and enthusiasm for game development.

## Preparing for the job market



### Taking a game development class

During your time in school taking the right classes to prepare you for what you want to do is important. Of course their are the required classes for graduating, but what can really help is taking electives that help you specialize in the field you want to be in. <br>
If your school offers it as an elective taking a game development class can help you get started, if not their are other options. Unity offers a free course on learning their game engine, and Unreal engine also has a begginer course choosing one of these options will help begin your process in stepping into the field.

### Participating in game jams

Game jams are an amazing way to practice making games and getting projects under your portfolio. They also allow you to show employers your range of skills and that you can work with a team on a creative project. To get started with game jams find one that fits your schedule so you can work on it without overexerting yourself, to find a game jam there are plenty of resources available like itch.io game jam calender all you have to do is search. 

### Working on personal projects

Projects similar to game jams are the best way to show off your skills in development and creativity. Personal projects allow you to create something that interests you while preparing for the job market, when looking at potential applicants employers would rather see that you are capable of doing the job through your portfolio than look at your list of skills on a resume. Getting started on a project can be difficult so starting off with a concept or something small, clear, and achievable in a short amount of time is a good start because it will more likely achieve completion. Once you've set a end goal and have an idea, you can take as much time as you please because there isn't a deadline for a personal project.

### Going to a job fair

Job fairs are the best way to meet potential employers and get yourself out there in the job market because of 2 reasons, 1 when talking to someone in person you avoid the possible middle man in the hiring process online that sends your resume and info through an automated system, and 2 you are able to make personal connections to people who work there and see what the work culture may be like if hired into a position offered. UW often has job fairs on campus, begin by researching the companies that will be present at the job fair. Bring multiple copies of your resume so you can hand it to each possible employer you wish, and dressing business casual is good.
